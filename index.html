Biota: Biology Innovation Outreach Teaching and Advancment

Welcome to BIOTA, where our dedication lies in imparting a passion for biology to all. We are committed to making STEM education engaging through meticulously designed competitions. Join us in dismantling barriers and fostering a world where biology is universally accessible. Together, let us ignite curiosity and collectively shape the future of science.

Why do we think early exposure to biology crucial? Introducing children to the marvels of the natural world sparks curiosity and nurtures future scientists, environmentalists, and healthcare professionals. Our mission is to empower young minds with knowledge, fostering a lifelong passion for biology and informed decision-making.
